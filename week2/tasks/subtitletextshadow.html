Motu Patlu Show

Motu Patlu is an Indian animated sitcom television series airing on Nickelodeon. The show features the adventures of Motu and Patlu, two friends living in Furfuri Nagar.

About the Show

The series is based on the comic characters of the same name created by Kripa Shankar Bhardwaj. It is one of the most popular animated series among children in India.

Characters

The main characters include Motu, who loves samosas and has a strong appetite, and Patlu, who is intelligent and often saves Motu from trouble.

Production and Reception

The show has received positive reviews for its humor and entertainment value, making it a favorite among kids and families alike.
